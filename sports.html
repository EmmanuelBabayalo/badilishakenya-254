Sports

Badilisha will be running a number of programs to promote sports development, through advocacy of a lottery scheme to fund and support professionalization of local sporting leagues in institutions, both primary and secondary schools.

The initiative will do this by ensuring introduction of professional coaching to foster the deepest talents and competencies. Establish a network of national academics for young people each one specializing in a particular sport. As the swahili saying "samaki mkunje angali mbichi."

Building capacity to facilitate a bid of hosting championship locally and internationally, will be our drive.
