Who We Are

Badilisha Kenya Initiative is a non-governmental organization based and established in Kenya with aim of providing a comprehensive empowerment in the Kenyan Community. Our key goal is seeing a transformed and an ethusiastic community, that fathers the understanding of self-employment, develops Entrepreneurial skills in both formal and non-formal settlements. This will be achieved through training programs, mentorship forums and resource support in their day to day economical activities.
