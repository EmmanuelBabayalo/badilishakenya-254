education

education being a foundation of economic development and an instrument tool in inculcating values and principles. The initiative focuses on offering educational empowerment, through bursaries to schools, estabishment of affordable communities' schools and vacational centers.

we also intend to address challenges relating to: Teachers development skills and knowledge by coming up with compentency training/ workshops, capacity building to improve service delivery in the education sector

Establishing and supporting rescue care centers for children in basic education and poor adults.(orphaned, widows, single parent and the less fortunate families in the society)

vision statement

to provide a holistic transformation of quality services and equitable empowerment to the community with aim of producing a first world class of oriented environment.

Youth empowerment

youth empowerment-the youth sector faces challenges like unemployment compounded by rapid population growth, with limited linkages between industry and training instition. badilisha aims at creating sustainable employment by comping up with projects like conversion of waste to wealth, linking the youth with youth enterprise development fund.

establish a structure that will advocate for creation of youth capital fund, that will anable youth access interest free business financial either individually or in groups without the required of traditional collateral.

development of youth (SACCOS) and providing grants to youth (ASALS) with clear assement basis.

mission statement

empowering the community with tools to engage in meaningful development activities to enhance their rate of employment, self-oriented and relevant mindset.

to network on resource mobilization and foster the spirit of sharing the resources realized and released.
