About us

badilisha kenya initiative is a non-governmental organization based and established in kenya with aims of providing a comprehensive empowerment to the Kenyan community, that fathers the understanding of self-employment, develops entrepreneurial skills in both formal and non formal settlements.

This will be achieved through training programs, mentorship forums and resource support in their day to day economical activities.

Objective

the main objective of this initiative is to enhance development of basic skills through public awareness campaigns, aiming at accelerating an inclusive economic growth. Enacting of higher living standards, sector that may lead into a double digit economical development peogram (EDP) growth rate.

specific objectives

to promote education in the community,to low the graphical level of unemployment and poverty through our gross investment plan,to elevate community awareness an emerging issues,
