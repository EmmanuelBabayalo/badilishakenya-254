community/society

since Kenya's independence, there has been a challenge of one man-show, where members of the community will work individually to achieve their own goals and objectives.

BKI is breaking this gap by bringing all member of the commmunity together to foster togetherness and cordial working relationships through creation of community business incubation center like community collaboration (C.C).

Development of community SACCOS and providing grants to joint community groups; Self Help groups, C.B.O will be used to foster unity in the community BKI will also organize community awareness and engagement forums that will bring in ideas and delibaration from various quarters.

health

the goal for the health sector is to provide care as to the constitution through the bill of rights. The initiative aims at educating the community on mortality rates pointing out issues that affect them directly e.g.the main cause of deaths; perinatal conditions(9.0%), HIV/AIDS(29.3%) maternal death remaining a major challenge by creation of awareness of members of the community accessing health facilities whenever they detect an illness.

registration of medical covers like (NHIF) will be much advocated by the initiatiive through our dispensing health care programs(DHP)
